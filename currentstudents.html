Thesis Guidelines

This guide has been prepared to help graduate students prepare their dissertation and thesis for acceptance by Ghazali Shafie Graduate School of Government (GSGSG), Universiti Utara Malaysia. The regulations contained within have been updated for the benefit of students, faculty advisors, departments and the Graduate School. Any questions about the contents of this guide should be directed to the Advisor(GSGSG Research Officer), Ghazali Shafie Graduate School of Government (GSGSG), Universiti Utara Malaysia.

The Format Advisor is concerned solely with the format of the manuscripts. It is the supervisor's responsibility to make certain that any dissertation or thesis submitted in partial fulfilment or fulfilment of a graduate degree at Ghazali Shafie Graduate School of Government (GSGSG), Universiti Utara Malaysia conforms to the regulations outlined in this guide. Responsibility for the content of any manuscript remains with the student and the faculty advisor(s).
